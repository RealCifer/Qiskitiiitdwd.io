Welcome to the IBM Qiskit Fall Fest at IIIT Dharwad

We’re thrilled to embark on this journey of learning about Quantum Computing, sponsored by IBM. This session is open to everyone, so feel free to bring friends—no prior knowledge is required! Join us to explore the basics of quantum computing, attend talks, and learn how to develop applications with Qiskit on real IBM quantum computers. Plus, don’t miss our hands-on challenge where you can apply your knowledge, collaborate with peers, and win exciting prizes! Get ready for a day of learning, innovation, and fun as we dive into the world of quantum technology!
